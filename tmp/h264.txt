H.264
	- MPEG-4 Part 10, Advanced Video Coding (MPEG-4 AVC)
	- a video coding format
	- MPEG LA administers licensing
		- free use of H.264 technologies to end users for streaming internet video
		- requiring royalties for commercial use of H.264 technologies
		- Cisco Systems would pay royalties on behalf of the users of binaries for its open source H.264 encoder.
